3D Character Controller

Bringing life to Basil the Basilisk with spring based physics and dynamic acceleration

Who's Basil?

I've been creating and leading a team to tackle our most ambitious project yet. Basil is an expressive 3D platformer character with fast feet and sick spins. Engineering her spring based movement, tools to control her jump arcs, and linking up her expressive animations and visual effects has been my main project for the last year. My team and I are working hard to finish the last bits of polish before publishing in December of this year!

Coming to Steam and itch.io this December!
